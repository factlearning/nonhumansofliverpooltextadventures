I love my job overseeing Liverpool and the River Mersey. I can see everything that goes on, not that I am nosey of course, but you should see the antics of the other folk, I could write a book! [[would you like to know more about me?]]I was the first part of Liverpool to be occupied by rich merchants then industry and workers houses, which unfortunately became slums and people were moved to suburbs. I also housed lots of small quarries, the sandstone being used for local buildings. [[look and see what I do now]]I host a heritage trail! I have a nature garden, two small lakes, woodland and meadowland and of course that wonderful view. [[come and see my famous little building]] Everton lock-up (Prince Rupert's Tower). Built using my sandstone in 1787 and used to keep criminals overnight. Now it's image is part of Everton Football Clubs emblem - how good is that?
